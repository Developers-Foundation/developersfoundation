MISSION STATEMENT

Every developer can make a difference.

We believe that every good cause deserves a great website. As a group an organization of college, university and highschool students we are constantly excited to building the next 'great thing'.

Developers

Spanning more than 10 faculties, our developers are funny, genuine and passionate about making a difference.

Designers

Designers are the lifeblood of every website. They work closely with developers to uniquely design your website.

Marketer

Our business and marketing students are carefully selected to handle your social media marketing, pro-bono consulting for your marketing objectives and operation challenges.

  • Application Submission
