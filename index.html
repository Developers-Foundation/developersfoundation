Mission Statement

Every developer can make a difference.

We believe that every good cause deserves a great website. As an organization of college, university and highschool students, we are constantly excited to building the next 'great thing'.

Developers

Spanning more than 10 faculties, our developers are funny, genuine and
passionate about making a difference.

Designers

Designers are the lifeblood of every website. They work closely with developers to
uniquely design your website.

Marketers

Our business and marketing students are carefully selected to handle your social
media marketing, pro-bono consulting for your marketing objectives and operation challenges.

How It Works

From Submitting an application to getting a great website.

  • Application Submission

    Fill out our 2 minute form so we can know more about you and the needs of your organization

  • Application reviewed

    We will evaluate the scope and goal of your submission.

  • Our team begins development

    Doing what we do best.

  • Iterate on user feedback

    Pilot website presented based on feedback.

  • Deliver the final application

    Launching the long awaited website.
