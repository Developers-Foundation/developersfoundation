What is Developers' Foundation?

Developers' Foundation is a non-profit/charity organization. Its main purpose is Developers’ Foundation is a NPO that mentors and teaches students how to develop software that can positively impact their development of skills in the area of technology. DF currently has 3 university chapters as of August 13th. Each chapter is responsible for impacting their local community by the means of education, training and software solutions.

What products or services do you offer?

We service Student Organizations at local universities for free, and approach charity organizations and small businesses with our services of building websites and software at a much cheaper price, compared to the industry. In regards to web design, we offer different services from a Landing page, multiple pages, Gallery Databases, and SEO's.

How do does Developers’ Foundation differ from other web-design and software developer firms?

As a student led organization, we attract great student talent from a vast variety of faculties. As a non-profit, all proceeds will be used to educate communities with software development and fund post-secondary scholarships for students wishing to pursue technology programs in colleges and universities

I am part of a small business or charitable organization and I am interested in your services. Who do I contact?

As a student led organization, please check if there is a Chapter present in your local University. You are strongly encouraged to send us an email if you have an inquiry. Members of the Headquarters chapter will follow up with you in less than two business days.
E-mail: sales@developersfoundation.ca

I am currently a post-secondary student, how do I start my own chapter?

We are always excited to welcome new chapters to our organization! Starting a new chapter requires dedication and commitment. We recommend recruiting most of your executive team before applying on our website.
We encouraged you to contact operations@developersfoundation.ca for any chapter establishment inquiries.

I’m currently a student at a University where a chapter currently exists, how can I join the chapter?

You are encouraged to seek out your local Chapter. Their respective Developers’ Foundation website will provide you with more information if applications are available.

Do I have to know how to web design or code to join?

Absolutely not! When starting, majority of our developers were unfamiliar with the processes of building a website. In fact, our experiences shows that it can be quickly picked up in less than a month of utilizing learning resources that we provide to all members. However, having previous experience with coding and programming languages is an asset.

Where are your headquarters located?

Developers’ Foundation does not have brick and mortar locations. Our Headquarters compose of 5 selected students from different Chapters across the province.

Our Chapters, and the organization as a whole enjoys hearing the positive impact that our efforts have had with our clients and our communities. These stories are why we volunteer our time and efforts, and we are honored if you would like to share your stories with us.
